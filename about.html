What is Agriculture?

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating plants and livestock. It plays an important role in the global economy.

With modern technologies like IoT, AI and drones, agriculture is becoming more efficient and sustainable.

Agriculture Information

Introduction

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating soil, growing crops, and raising animals for food and other products. It is one of the oldest occupations and supports human life.

Importance

Agriculture provides food, employment, raw materials for industries, and contributes to the economic development of a country.

Types of Agriculture

Subsistence farming, commercial farming, organic farming, mixed farming, and mechanized farming are major types of agriculture.

Agricultural Activities

Important activities include soil preparation, sowing, irrigation, fertilization, pest control, and harvesting crops.

Major Crops

Common crops include rice, wheat, maize, cotton, sugarcane, barley, and pulses.

Modern Technology

Modern agriculture uses drip irrigation, greenhouses, agricultural drones, and smart irrigation systems.

Challenges

Climate change, water shortage, soil degradation, and pest attacks are major challenges in agriculture.

Conclusion

Agriculture is the backbone of the economy and ensures food security. Modern technology helps improve productivity.
