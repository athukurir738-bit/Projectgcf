Different Types of Crops

Food Crops

Food crops are grown for human consumption such as rice, wheat, maize and pulses.

Cash Crops

Cash crops are grown to sell in markets like cotton, sugarcane, coffee and tea.

Plantation Crops

These crops are grown on large farms such as tea, rubber, coconut and coffee.

Fiber Crops

Fiber crops are used to produce textiles and ropes such as cotton and jute.

Oil Crops

Oil crops produce edible oil like sunflower, mustard, soybean and groundnut.

Horticulture Crops

These include fruits, vegetables and flowers such as mango, apple, tomato and rose.
